ScrollPhase Demo

Experience ASDR-style envelope transitions as you scroll. Each content block transitions through Attack, Sustain, Decay, and Release phases.

Attack Phase

When this item enters the viewport, it's in the Attack phase. It starts small and faded, gradually becoming more visible.

ENTERING

Rising Phase

As the item continues into view, it enters the Rising phase. The blur clears and opacity increases, preparing for full visibility.

RISING

Sustain Phase

At the sweet spot of the viewport, the item is in Sustain phase. Full size, full opacity, no blur - perfect readability.

FULL

Decay Phase

As you continue scrolling, the item enters the Decay phase. It begins to fade and blur slightly, signaling its departure.

FADING

Release Phase

Finally, the item enters the Release phase as it leaves the viewport. Small, faded, and blurred - gracefully exiting.

EXITING

Pinning Support

ScrollPhase supports pinning items to the top or bottom of the viewport. As items cross threshold boundaries, they can stick in place.

PINNABLE

Smooth Transitions

All transitions are GPU-accelerated using CSS transforms and opacity. This ensures buttery-smooth 60fps animations even with many items.

PERFORMANT

Event Callbacks

ScrollPhase fires events on state changes, allowing you to hook into the scroll lifecycle for custom behaviors and analytics.
